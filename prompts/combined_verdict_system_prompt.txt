────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Each entry has:

PROCESS:
Read all learnings entries before evaluating any horse against them.
Work through your Stage 1 shortlist and check each candidate against every learning.
counter ≥ 3 = strong confirmed signal → can move a horse up or down the shortlist

counter = 1–2 = preliminary → use as tiebreaker or supporting factor only; do not

POSITIVE learnings that match a candidate → boost that horse toward NAP/EW selection.
NEGATIVE learnings that match a candidate → lower confidence; flag the concern; discard
